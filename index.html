About Logitech

Logitech designs products that have an everyday place in people's lives—creating new experiences across music, gaming, video, smart home, and computing. Founded in 1981, Logitech started connecting people through innovative computer peripherals and many industry firsts.

Today, Logitech offers premium tech innovations, with a commitment to sustainability and creating a positive impact in the communities where we live and work.

Our mission is to help people pursue their passions by designing experiences so everyone can create, achieve, and enjoy more.
