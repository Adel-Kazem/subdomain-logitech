About Us

物語 Our Story

Founded in Tokyo in 2018, MONO was born from a desire to create t-shirts that embody the essence of Japanese minimalism — pieces that are timeless, functional, and beautiful in their simplicity.

We focus on three key principles: quality materials, thoughtful design, and responsible production. Each t-shirt is crafted from premium organic cotton or linen blends, designed with intention, and produced in small batches to minimize waste.

Our design philosophy draws inspiration from the Japanese concepts of "Ma" (間) — the meaningful space between elements — and "Shibui" (渋い) — simple, subtle, and unobtrusive beauty.
