Our Story

Bringing Color to Life

CHROMATIC began with a simple idea: to create vibrant, high-quality t-shirts that help people express their unique personalities through color and design.

Founded in 2018 by a group of designers passionate about sustainable fashion, we've grown from a small studio in Portland to a beloved brand with customers worldwide. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and sustainability guides everything we do.

Each CHROMATIC t-shirt is crafted using premium organic cotton and eco-friendly dyes, ensuring both comfort and reduced environmental impact. Our designs range from subtle minimalist patterns to bold artistic expressions, each created to help you showcase your true colors.
